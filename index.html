Toronto Raptors NBA Champions 2019

In 2019 the Toronto Raptors beat the Golden State Warriors in a 4-2 series earning their first ever NBA championship title. This was huge for major sports in Toronto as it was the first time in 25 years that a Toronto team appeared in the championship round from any of the four major North American Professional Sports Leagues since the Toronto Blue Jays win in the 1993 World Series. The Raptors were led to victory by coach Nick Nurse (reciepient of the NBA Coach of the Year award in 2020).
